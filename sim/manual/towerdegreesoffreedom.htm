Tower Degrees of Freedom

 

Sim42 automatically and continually monitors the degrees of freedom of all unit operations, including the distillation tower unit operation. Traditional process simulators usually separate the tower unit operation from the main solver creating the need for a special algorithm for degrees of freedom monitoring as well as displaying such information to the user.

Sim42 behaves quite differently. The tower unit operation is seen as just another unit operation, and the degrees of freedom calculations are performed automatically depending on the tower configuration you are creating, of course in an interactive manner. Depending on the specified values that you provide in the tower material or energy ports or any attached material and energy streams, the tower unit operation will attempt to solve the particular configuration. This provides you with unparalleled flexibility in the specification of distillation towers.

The number of degrees of freedom in any tower can be summarized as the result of the following expression:

DF

number of degrees of freedom

SVD

number of side vapor draws

SLD

number of side liquid draws

ED

number of energy draws

EF

number of energy feeds

For example, if you have a simple absorption tower, SVD = 1, SLD = 1 and therefore you do not have any degrees of freedom. As soon as the top and bottoms feeds are completely specified the tower can be run.
