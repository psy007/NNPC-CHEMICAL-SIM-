Introduction

 

Welcome to Sim42, the powerful open source chemical process simulator. Sim42 was designed to provide you with a state of the art interactive process simulation environment containing advanced unit operations such as distillation towers, compressors and balances coupled with an advanced solver capable of supporting bidirectional information flow, multiple flow sheets and automatic convergence of recycle streams.

Sim42 embraces the open source concept, where you are free to study the source code behind the scenes and modify it to fit any specific purpose you may have in mind.
