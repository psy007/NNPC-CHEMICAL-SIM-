Tower Convergence Criteria

 

In general inner errors are normalized in some manner depending upon the type of variable. Heat errors are the most common of course and these are roughly the heat imbalance divided by the total heat flows into and out of the stage. Temperatures are the difference from the spec in K divided by 500. Flow errors are divided by the sum of the total feed flows to the entire tower.
