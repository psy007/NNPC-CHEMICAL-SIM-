LiqLiqExt – sim.unitop.LiqLiqExt.LiqLiqEx

 

The LiqLiqExt unit operation models a liquid-liquid counter-current extractor. Note that this unit operation is derived from a Flowsheet unit operation containing normal flash and balance operations and just uses the normal recycle solver to solve the concurrent flows.
